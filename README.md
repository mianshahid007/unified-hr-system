# Unified HR Management System

A comprehensive Human Resource Management and Workforce Operations platform built with React.

This project demonstrates how organizations can centralize HR operations, employee lifecycle management, finance tracking, recruitment workflows, asset management, and employee support services into a single unified system.

**Features**

**Employee Management**
- Employee Profiles
- Department Management
- Designations & Roles
- Employee Search & Filtering
- Employee Status Management

**Attendance Management**
- Daily Attendance Tracking
- Monthly Attendance Calendar
- Present / Absent / Half Day Tracking
- Attendance Reporting
- Attendance Export

**Leave Management**
- Leave Requests
- Leave Approval Workflow
- Sick Leave
- Vacation Leave
- Personal Leave
- Leave History

**Payroll Management**
- Salary Records
- Allowances
- Tax Deductions
- Loan Deductions
- Payroll Processing

**Recruitment Management**
- Job Openings
- Candidate Tracking
- Interview Pipeline
- Candidate Scoring
- Recruitment Analytics

**Performance Management
**- Goal Tracking
- KPI Monitoring
- Performance Reviews
- Employee Ratings
- Progress Monitoring

**Asset Management**
- Laptop Tracking
- Device Assignment
- Asset Inventory
- Asset Status Monitoring

**Helpdesk & Support**
- Employee Support Tickets
- IT Requests
- HR Requests
- Finance Requests
- Ticket Resolution Workflow

**Employee Onboarding**
- Onboarding Checklist
- Welcome Tasks
- Documentation Tracking
- Training Management

**Finance & Cash Flow**
- Income Tracking
- Expense Tracking
- Budget Categories
- Financial Dashboard
- Cash Flow Analytics

**Reporting & Analytics**
- Workforce Analytics
- Attendance Reports
- Financial Insights
- Recruitment Metrics
- Dashboard KPIs
